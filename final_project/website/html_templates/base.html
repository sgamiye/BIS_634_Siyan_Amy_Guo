Fetal Health Check!


Fetal health is an area that deserves attention because it is something that can be constantly monitored which should be relatively easier and more accessible to manage.





However, the U.S. fetal mortality rate was 5.70 fetal deaths at 20 or more weeks of gestation per 1,000 live births and fetal deaths, and often occurs at low resource setting and most of them can be prevented.
Therefore, if the predictability is effective, such model can be put in use in a more accessible way than regular healthcare visit, it could be incorporated into telemedicine in order to provide information and referral on fetal health status with parameter values provided.
